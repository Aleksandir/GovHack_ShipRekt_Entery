The S.S Admella crashed on Carpenters Rocks Reef, North West of Cape Northumberland on August 6th 1859.
Weight: 209 tonnes
Master: Hugh McEwan
Traveling From: Adelaide
Destination: To Melbourne
Passengers: 113
Cargo:
  • 93tns Copper
  • Seven Horses
  • General merchandise

86 men and women including
fifteen children died

26 Men survived
1 women survived
2 Sailors survived
